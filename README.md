# ProductVisualizer

Product Visualizer & E-Commerce App is an innovative mobile and web application that combines immersive product visualization with a complete online shopping experience. The platform allows users to explore, customize, and visualize products in detail before making a purchase — bridging the gap between imagination and reality.

Next update :- 
- will feature a 3D/AR-based product visualizer, enabling customers to view products from all angles, zoom in for fine details, and even place items virtually in their environment (for example, furniture in a room or accessories on a model). 
- Once users are satisfied with the product visualization, they can seamlessly transition to the e-commerce module to add items to their cart, make secure payments, and track orders.
